Speed ({{rsstatspeedtxt}})
Each point in speed increases attack speed and gives attack piercing.
Maximum attack speed is 10 attacks per second.
Attack Piercing means you can level up a number of levels at the same time if your damage is high enough to cover 2 or more levels at the same time. In order to get more attack piercing I suggest you invest more and more in speed to find out.
Maximum Levels Peirced on overkill is {{rsattackspeedPierce+rsSkillAPPoints}}.
